Runwen Yao, Ph.D.

Postdoctoral researcher with interdisciplinary training spanning cell biology, RNA biology, biochemistry, biophysics, and advanced imaging.

Ph.D. training with Dr. Ling-Ling Chen at the Chinese Academy of Sciences, focused on nucleolar organization and RNA-mediated regulation of nuclear condensates.

Currently a postdoctoral scientist in the laboratory of Dr. Michael K. Rosen at HHMI/UT Southwestern, investigating how mesoscale physical properties of biomolecular condensates encode biochemical function.
